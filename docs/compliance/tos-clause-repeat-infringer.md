## Clause B — Repeat-Infringer Termination Policy

### B.1 Copyright Strike System

In accordance with our obligations under applicable copyright law, including the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (17 U.S.C. § 512(i)) and, where applicable, the EU Copyright Directive (2019/790), Syra maintains and enforces a repeat-infringer policy.

When Syra receives a valid copyright infringement notice regarding Content you have uploaded, or when Syra reasonably determines that your Content infringes a third party's copyright, Syra may:

(a) Remove or disable access to the infringing Content;

(b) Issue a copyright strike to your account; and

(c) Notify you of the action taken and the basis for it.

### B.2 Termination Threshold

**Your account will be permanently terminated upon receipt of three (3) copyright strikes.** Upon termination:

(a) Your account and all associated artist profiles will be permanently suspended;

(b) Your upload privileges will be revoked;

(c) All Content associated with your account will be removed from the platform; and

(d) You will be prohibited from creating new accounts on Syra.

Syra reserves the right to reduce the strike threshold or to terminate accounts immediately, without prior strikes, in cases of egregious, willful, or large-scale infringement.

### B.3 Counter-Notification

If you believe that Content was removed as a result of a mistake or misidentification, you may submit a counter-notification in accordance with the DMCA (17 U.S.C. § 512(g)) or applicable local law. Counter-notifications must be submitted to Syra's Designated DMCA Agent at:

```
dmca@oxy.so
```

A valid counter-notification must include:
- Identification of the Content that was removed and its prior location on Syra;
- A statement under penalty of perjury that you believe the removal was a mistake or misidentification;
- Your contact information (name, address, telephone, email); and
- A statement consenting to jurisdiction of the federal district court for your address (or, outside the US, any judicial district in which Syra may be found).

### B.4 Strike Appeals

You may contest a strike by contacting `dmca@oxy.so` with documentation supporting your claim of ownership or licensed use. Strike appeals are reviewed at Syra's sole discretion. Submission of an appeal does not suspend the accumulation of strikes or the enforcement of the termination threshold.

### B.5 Reinstatement

Account reinstatement following termination under this policy is not guaranteed and is subject to Syra's sole discretion. Reinstatement requests may be submitted to `legal@oxy.so` with supporting documentation. Syra is under no obligation to grant reinstatement.
